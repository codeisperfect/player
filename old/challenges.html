Challenges to your focus

What is stopping you from focusing fully on your game?


1- Doubt

                            

My mind routinely has doubts about some important aspect of my game such as my ability & talent, technique, equipment, coach, etc


2- Confusion /indecision?

3- Laziness (lack of regularity/consistency       

 

I don’t think I am really training hard enough.I waste lot of time in day dreaming about results but not actually working towards them. My self-discipline is not good enough. I am not regular at my training.


4- Carelessness/negligence

 

I make careless mistakes due to absent mindedness or because I get over confident at times.It affectsmy performance.Though I say my sports is really a top priority for me, but my behavior does not show that


5- Lack of detachment (too much emotional involvement with results)

 

I am concerned so much about my results that sports has become an emotionally draining experience for me?I am not able to deal with the emotions well.


6- Instability of mind?

I find it difficult to hold my attention on one thing at a time even for short time. My focus waver a lot during training and matches


7- Hyperactivity( Too many fidgety movements, not attained a base)

If I am asked to sit down quietly, I find it difficult to sit down in one place without moving. I am hyperactive and tend to move a lot.

9- Memory

The memories of previous matches bother me a lot and interfere with my current performance. I find it difficult to forget the bad matches and move on.

My mind imagines/thinks negative events, results or consequences powerful enough to disturb my focus on the game
